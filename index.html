FAQs

Do you sign an NDA?

Yes we do. You can completely rely on our developers to keep your idea safe and private. We will, on completion of the project, hand over the app and the source code to you.

What are the stages involved in the mobile app development process?

There are three stages in our mobile app development process: The first stage is of planning in which we, after understanding your business and your objectives behind developing a mobile app, will wireframe a solution. The second stage is the design and development stage wherein we will create designs, character models (2D or 3D), sound effects and will test and remove the bugs and errors in the app, if any. The third and final stage marks the launch of the app followed by the marketing of the app.

What are the cost ranges that you offer?

Again, the cost of the app also depends on its complexity and your preferences. Other contributing factors are the types of graphics used, sound effects, the platform chosen, etc. To know about the estimated price of your project, kindly send us your requirements and we will get back to you shortly.

How much time does the whole project take from ideation to building the app?

From 3-4 weeks till 9-10 months, it totally depends on the complexities of the app.

What are the platforms on which you develop apps?

At Monkhub, we provide native mobile app development solutions on iOS and Android. We also offer hybrid/cross-platform mobile app development solutions on HTML5.

I only want to enhance my existing app? Can I get it done?

Yes, of course. You can either partner with us to develop an app right from the beginning or just to enhance the functionality of your app. You can avail our mobile app development services entirely or individually in either form. In any case, we promise to deliver high-quality apps.

Will you provide assistance after the completion of my project?

Yes we provide assistance and help you keep your app safe and secured. We have monthly plans for your app’s support and maintenance.

What tools you use for project management?

We use Basecamp, Jira and a few others. We are also open to any other tool that client suggests.

What are the extra efforts to build an app for tablet?

A tablet has a higher screen resolution and size in comparison to a Smartphone. The only effort required in tablet app development will be the adjustment of screen resolution.

How will I know the status of my projects?

Each of our mobile app development projects is allocated to one of our experienced project manager. He always keeps communicating with the clients during the working hours via Skype, email and phone and acts as a single point of contact informing our clients about the progress of their projects.

Do you provide any warranty?

Yes, we provide 90 days of free support and maintenance post-delivery of the project. After this period, we charge nominally on a monthly basis.

Do I need to test my app?

No, we have an in-house testing team who will test your app and make it bug free.

How do you manage the changes in the project scope?

We always believe in flexibility in work and hence accommodate all possible changes to scope during the course of the project. If we find that the changes requested are beyond the original scope, we communicate with you and explain the cost and time impact before going ahead with the new scope.

Will I own the source code?

Yes, you will have 100% ownership of your source code.

Will my app be submitted to the app stores?

Yes, we take care of submitting your app to Google Play Store and Apple App Store.

Do you provide marketing services?

Yes, we have our marketing and communications team who can provide you with a comprehensive marketing plan and execute it for your app.

What is your preferred way of communication?

We can communicate with you via emails, phone calls, or Skype. We also have our dedicated help desk services to respond to your queries within a few hours.

Do you provide app maintenance services?

Yes, we provide app maintenance services at a nominal cost after the launch of the app.

What are the benefits of mobile app development?

Mobile app development helps in enhancing user engagement, providing personalized services, increasing accessibility, and improving the overall user experience, which in turn leads to better customer satisfaction and business growth.

How do you ensure the quality of the app?

We follow a stringent quality assurance process that includes multiple stages of testing and validation to ensure the app meets all quality standards and functions as expected.

Can you integrate third-party services into the app?

Yes, we can integrate various third-party services and APIs into your app to enhance its functionality and provide a better user experience.

How do you handle app updates?

We provide regular app updates to ensure it remains compatible with the latest operating systems and includes new features or improvements based on user feedback and technological advancements.

Do you offer cross-platform app development?

Yes, we offer cross-platform app development services using frameworks like React Native, Flutter, and Xamarin, allowing your app to run smoothly on both iOS and Android platforms.

Can you help with app store optimization (ASO)?

Yes, we offer app store optimization (ASO) services to improve your app's visibility and ranking in app stores, helping you attract more downloads and users.

What is your approach to UI/UX design?

We follow a user-centric approach to UI/UX design, focusing on creating intuitive, engaging, and visually appealing interfaces that provide a seamless user experience.

How do you handle security and privacy concerns?

We implement robust security measures and follow industry best practices to ensure your app's data is secure and user privacy is protected.

Can you help with app monetization strategies?

Yes, we can help you develop effective app monetization strategies, such as in-app purchases, subscription models, and advertising, to generate revenue from your app.

Do you offer support for wearable devices?

Yes, we offer development services for wearable devices, ensuring your app is compatible with smartwatches and other wearables, providing a seamless experience across all devices.

What is your pricing model?

Our pricing model is flexible and can be tailored to your project's requirements. We offer both fixed-price and time-and-materials pricing models, depending on the nature and scope of the project.

How do you handle app scalability?

We design and develop apps with scalability in mind, ensuring they can handle increasing user loads and can be easily updated with new features as your business grows.
